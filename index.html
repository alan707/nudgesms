Welcome to NudgeSMS

NudgeSMS helps to massively reduce energy consumption by sending text messages to State of CA employees. The text messages contain actionable energy conservation measures to do for 1 hour.

Concept

NudgeSMS is a text messaging application that aims to improve energy and water conservation practices by sending text messages to state employees.

How do I register?

Send a text message to (760) 452-8548 with your building code.
